Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our AI-powered compliance platform for Chartered Accountants.

How does the agentic AI work?

Our AI agents autonomously read documents, extract data, reconcile records, and generate compliance outputs — all without step-by-step human instruction. You supervise, AI executes.

What's the difference between Web View and Semi-Chat View?

Web View gives you structured panels with checklists and forms. Semi-Chat View lets you interact with our AI GPT in a conversation. Both modes produce the same outputs.

Is the platform compliant with Indian regulations?

Yes — all templates reference Indian Contract Act, Companies Act, FEMA, RBI circulars, and ICAI standards. CA certificates support UDIN integration.

How do I integrate Tally with ComplianceAI?

Our one-click Tally sync pulls data from Tally Prime/ERP 9 running on port 9000. No plugins needed — just select the company and sync.

Which compliance modules are available?

GST Filing, Income Tax, Audit, CA Certificates, Agreements, Data Entry, Payroll, Company Law, Accounting, and more — with new AI modules added regularly.

Can I request a custom AI agent?

Absolutely! Tell us your workflow — like auto-sending emails, reading FIRC attachments, or smart document sorting — and we'll build a custom AI agent tailored to your practice.
