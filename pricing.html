Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

What happens after my 30-day free trial?

After your trial ends, you'll need to choose a paid plan to continue. Your data is safe — we hold it for 30 more days so you can upgrade without losing anything.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and bank transfers. All secured through Razorpay.

Do you offer discounts for CA firms?

Yes — volume discounts for 50+ clients and special pricing for ICAI members. Contact our sales team.
