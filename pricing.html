Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Does ComplianceAI have a free plan?

Yes! Our Free plan is free forever and includes up to 5 clients, basic GST tools, AI GPT assistant, document reader, and 2GB cloud storage. No credit card required to get started.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed through Razorpay with full encryption.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% on all paid plans. Toggle the billing switch above to see annual pricing.

Do you offer special pricing for ICAI members or CA firms?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for firms with 50+ clients and special pricing for ICAI members. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.
