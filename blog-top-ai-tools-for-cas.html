2. AI-Powered GST Reconciliation Tools

Best for: CAs who primarily handle GST compliance

Within Suvidha AI, the GST reconciliation engine is the most-used feature. It handles 6 types of reconciliation:

  • GSTR-1 vs GSTR-3B — Tax liability matching with ASMT-10 risk assessment
  • GSTR-2B vs GSTR-3B — ITC claimed vs ITC available (Table 4A analysis)
  • GSTR-2B vs Purchase Register — Invoice-level matching with categorization
  • IMS vs Purchase Register — Input Management System reconciliation
  • E-Invoice vs Sales Register — Outward supply verification
  • GSTR-1 vs E-Invoice — Filing vs invoicing cross-check

3. AI Document Reader & OCR

Best for: Converting paper documents into digital data

Suvidha AI's document reader can parse invoices, bank statements, financial statements, and any other document. Upload a photo or PDF — get structured, usable data in seconds.

4. Blocked Credit Analyzer

Best for: Ensuring ITC compliance under Section 17(5)

This AI tool scans your client's ITC claims and flags invoices where credit may be blocked under Section 17(5) of the CGST Act. Categories include:

  • Motor vehicles and conveyances
  • Food and beverages, outdoor catering
  • Beauty treatment, health services
  • Club memberships
  • Personal consumption goods

5. HSN/SAC Code Plotter

Best for: Correct classification of goods and services

Wrong HSN codes = wrong tax rates = compliance issues. Suvidha AI's HSN Plotter uses AI to suggest the correct code based on product description, with confidence scoring.

6. CA Certificate Generator

Best for: Fast, accurate certificate issuance

  • FEMA Certification
  • Bank/Loan Certificate
  • Turnover Certificate

7. GSTIN Bulk Verification Tool

Best for: Supplier due diligence and ITC protection
