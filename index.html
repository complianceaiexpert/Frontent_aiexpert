Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Who is this platform designed for? ▼
ComplianceAI Expert is built for Chartered Accountants, tax consultants, legal professionals, and financial advisors across India. Whether you're a solo practitioner or part of a large firm, our platform scales with you.
Is my client data safe and private? ▼
Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption and never store client data on our servers. All processing happens in-memory and data is discarded after your session. Your client's confidential information remains confidential.
Does it integrate with Tally? ▼
Yes! We have a native desktop connector that syncs directly with Tally Prime. Import your financial data with a single click — no manual data entry, no errors.
What AI model powers the platform? ▼
We use the latest GPT models combined with our proprietary compliance knowledge base. The AI understands Indian tax law, GST regulations, and FEMA guidelines to provide accurate, context-aware assistance.
Can I try it for free? ▼
Yes! Sign up for a free account and explore the platform. No credit card required. You'll get access to core features including client management, basic reconciliation tools, and AI assistance.
